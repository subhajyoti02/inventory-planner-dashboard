Forecasting Method


Forecasting for non-seasonal items. Computed from average sales during the period - , adjusted for trend if/when a sales pattern is recognized for the last months.

Combine Sales/Stock

Combine sales (and/or stock) from other variants to adjust the forecast. Use when the new variant has no sales history and is expected to have the same demand as old variants. No impact on the old variants.
